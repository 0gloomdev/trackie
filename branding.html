Social Preview Setup

  1. Go to your GitHub repository Settings
  2. Scroll to "Social Preview" section
  3. Click "Edit" and upload the github-thumbnail.svg or convert to PNG

Design Guidelines

  1. Always use on dark backgrounds (#060E20) for best glassmorphism effect
  2. Maintain minimum 20% breathing space around the logo
  3. Use the gradient palette consistently across all materials
  4. For best results, use SVG format when possible
